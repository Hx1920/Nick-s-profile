The project I am thinking about is to make a Side-scrolling video game on mobile phones. However instead of a horizontal plane like most on the market, mine will be a curved surface, and the idea is a man running on the surface on the moon. For summary, the players just need to touch their phones’ screen to control the character to either jump or squat to overcome the the terrain and the Meteorite to arrive the checkpoints. There will be ten levels and the difficulty will increase with the increase of the levels. Since the design of the game is very simple and it is familiar with most of the Side-scrolling video game you can see everywhere, I will design the game to be extremely difficult as its feature

As a game lover, I really hope that the first project I make in the course is a game, because I think is game bring me my interest in IT, therefore it will be a very memorable thing to create a game. The idea of the game is motivate by the game you can play on google page when there chrome cannot connect to the internet. Although it is just an extremely simple game even without colors, it is still attractive to me and I cannot stop clicking the dinosaur to play once when that page come out.

The game I am going to design will be called “Astronaut”, as the character players need to control is an astronaut. This is going to be a Side-scrolling video game with extremely simple control method. The astronaut in the game will stay at the same position on the screen all the way and the background which is a little curved part of the moon surface with pits and  Meteorite in the sky will keep rotating anticlockwise to make the astronaut appear to running on the surface of the moon. The players just need to tap their phone screens to control him to jump or squat depend on the conditions to avoid the pit and the Meteorite. While play the game, there will be just two control, which is to tap the left half side of the phone screen to squat or tap the right half side to jump. Ten levels of the game is divided into four stages. Whenever the astronaut drop into the pit or collide with the Meteorite, the player need to start from level one again. The first three levels are included in the the first stage which will just include some pits where players need to jump through. As the level increase from one to three, the speed of the rotation of the moon will get faster and the there will also be more pits that need to avoid falling, which require a rapid reaction of control on players. In stage two which include from level four to six, floating Meteorite at different level above the moon surface start appearing, so the players not only need to deal with the terrain but also need to either jump or squat depend on the hight of the floating Meteorite to avoid making contact with them. Like in stage one, the speed the rotation of the moon will increase and the number of floating Meteorite denser to enhance the difficulty. At the third stage which include from level seven to nine, flying Meteorite towards the astronaut will also be added, therefore player will see the Meteorite flying to the astronaut in different speed, and really need to think of how to dodge all of them in a half a second. At this stage, all the element including pits, floating Meteorite and flying Meteorite have appeared. The increase of difficulty in this stage will be similar to stage one and two which are by speeding up the rotation of the moon and the element. The last stage in level ten, which will be the boss of the game. With all the previous elements remained, an extremely dense Meteorite bullet screen will be added. The player need to try their best to avoid all of them and pay attention to the pits on the surface as well. At the time when the astronaut arrive the checkpoint which is a rocket in level ten, there will be an ending cg of him jumping into the the rocket and flying back to earth.
